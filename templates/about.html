About

The Idea of the IRiddle game is to display 20 riddles picked from a list of the top 100 riddles.

The user is then asked to guess the correct answer.

The user will receive 10 points for every question answered correctly and 3 points will be removed for every incorrect answer

Your score will be displayed on the scores page when the game is completed along with previous scores from other users.

Each of your incorrect answers will be displayed so you dont make the same mistake twice.
